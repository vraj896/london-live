Privacy policy

Last updated 12 June 2026

NEXT BUS shows live London transport arrivals. It is built to know as little about you as possible.

What the app stores

  • Saved stops, theme choice, and dismissed banners are stored in your browser's local storage, on your device only. They are never sent anywhere.
  • The app has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. We run no servers and keep no databases.

Location

If you allow location access, your coordinates are used on your device to find nearby stops and to centre the map. They are sent only as part of the request to Transport for London's API to fetch stops around you, and are never stored by us. You can use the app fully without location via Search.

Third-party services the app talks to

Like any website, these services see your IP address when the app requests data from them:

  • Transport for London — live arrival and stop data
  • postcodes.io — converting a postcode you type into coordinates
  • CARTO / OpenStreetMap — map tiles
  • Google Fonts and cdnjs — fonts and the map library

Changes & contact

If this policy changes, the date above will be updated. Questions: open an issue at github.com/vraj896/london-live.
